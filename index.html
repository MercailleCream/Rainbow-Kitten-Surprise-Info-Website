About the Band

Rainbow Kitten Surprise formed in North Carolina in 2013 by Darrick Keller and Ela Melo. They got their start as an indie band delving into alternative rock. Some of their albums are "RKS", "How to: Friend, Love, Freefall," and most recently, "Love Hate Music Box."

As of September 30th, the four members of the band are (from left to right) Ethan Goodpaster, Ela Melo, Darrick "Bozzy" Keller, and Jess Haney. Rainbow Kitten Surprise is currently touring as of September 9th of 2024; they will be snaking through the United States until November 25th.
